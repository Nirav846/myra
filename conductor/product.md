# Product Guide: MYRA (Myra Yield & Research Analytics)

## Vision
To build an elite, local-first institutional stock screener for NSE India traders and investors, providing real-time technical analysis, AI-powered predictions, and professional-grade data management.

## Goals
- Provide 40+ specialized technical and fundamental scanners.
- Deliver real-time alerts via Telegram integration.
- Maintain a thread-safe, high-performance data lake using DuckDB.
- Facilitate professional trading strategies (VCP, RSI, MACD, etc.).

## Key Features
- **Smart Scanning:** VCP detection, breakout identification, and custom piped strategies.
- **Gold Standard Ranking:** Global "Best First" result processor (Stage 2 > Stars > Money Flow).
- **Categorized Discovery:** Strategies grouped by Technical, Institutional, and Value factors.
- **Mission Control:** Responsive, live full-screen dashboard with real-time market intelligence and dynamic background sync progress.
- **AI-Powered:** Nifty trend prediction (3-Day Forecast) using XGBoost ensembles.
- **Institutional Core:** NSE Bhavcopy delivery data tracking with Smart Money Scores and Relative Delivery Volume (RDV).
- **Institutional Deal Tracking:** Integrated Bulk & Block deal momentum scanner.
- **Support Reversals:** Detection of multi-year price floors with institutional absorption.
- **Advanced VSA:** Volume Spread Analysis weighted by institutional delivery.
- **Advanced Math:** ATR expansion, Bollinger Bands, and trendline support detection.
- **Persistent Intelligence:** Delta-computed technicals (SMA, ATR, VWAP).

## Target Users
- Professional Day Traders & Swing Traders in the NSE India market.
- Institutional research analysts seeking local-first data analytics.
- Investors looking for data-driven, rule-based screening.
