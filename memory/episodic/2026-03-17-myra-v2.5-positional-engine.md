# Episode: The MYRA Positional Evolution (v2.5)
**Date**: 2026-03-17
**Status**: Architecture v2.5 Deployed (Strategic Holding Era)

## 1. Achievement: The Positional Engine
We successfully upgraded MYRA from a swing-biased tool to a robust positional intelligence center (v2.5). This transition was driven by the need for relative market strength analysis and long-term trend stability.

## 2. Core v2.5 Upgrades
1.  **Dynamic Relative Scoring**: Replaced fixed thresholds (e.g., ROCE > 15) with a **Percentile-Based Ranking** system. Stocks are now scored relative to the entire market universe.
2.  **Seven-Factor Scorer**:
    - **Trend Structure (25%)**: Long-term alignment (SMA 50 > 150 > 200).
    - **Trend Stability (15%)**: Consistency of price holding above moving averages.
    - **Delivery Accumulation (20%)**: Percentile-ranked delivery volume.
    - **Liquidity (10%)**: Safety check for tradability.
    - **Base Formation (10%)**: Volatility compression detection for timing.
    - **Fundamentals (20%)**: Dynamic Growth and Quality ranking.
3.  **Drawdown & Regime Awareness**:
    - Implemented a **Soft Drawdown Filter** to penalize broken charts while allowing for quality pullbacks.
    - Standardized **Market Regime Adjustments** based on India VIX psychology.

## 3. Technical Integration
- **Librarian (v25)**: Expanded Turbo-SQL precompute to include SMA 200, Stability windows (60d), and Drawdown metrics.
- **Engine (v12.5)**: Updated workers to pass full technical DNA (Indicator Cache) to the final results payload.
- **Screener (v2.5)**: Orchestrator now automatically applies v2.5 scoring and ranks candidates for 1-24 month holdings.
- **Results Manager (v1.6)**: Updated UI to highlight the new `MYRA_Score_v25` with dynamic coloring.

## 4. Conclusion
MYRA v2.5 is now fully operational as an elite institutional screener. It identifies future leaders early by detecting quiet accumulation and structural trend depth, while remaining resilient to missing fundamental data.
